Data Deletion Request

Effective date: February 12, 2026

You can request deletion of your Whistle account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to support@whistlesports.win with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your Whistle username
  • The email address associated with your account (if any)
  • Any additional details that help us verify ownership

What We Delete

We will delete your account profile, login identifiers, and related app activity. We may retain limited information if required by law or for security and fraud prevention.

Timeframe

We aim to complete deletion within 30 days of verification.
